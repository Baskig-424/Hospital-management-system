What is e-ICU?

The e-ICU (Electronic Intensive Care Unit) is a modern healthcare system that allows medical experts to monitor and treat critically ill patients remotely using advanced technology. It brings expert intervention to critically unwell patients even in the remotest parts of India.

Remote Patient Monitoring

Doctors and specialists can monitor ICU patients in real time through advanced digital systems. Vital signs and medical data are continuously tracked to ensure immediate medical attention.

Expert Medical Support

Experienced critical care specialists are available 24/7 to assist local doctors and medical teams in managing serious patient conditions.

Improved Emergency Response

The e-ICU system helps hospitals respond quickly to emergencies by providing instant access to expert guidance and medical decision support.

Healthcare for Remote Areas

Patients living in rural or remote locations can receive high-quality ICU care without needing to travel long distances to major hospitals.

How e-ICU Works

  1. Patient is admitted to the ICU in a hospital.
  2. Medical devices collect real-time patient data.
  3. The data is transmitted to a central monitoring center.
  4. Specialist doctors analyze the patients condition remotely.
  5. Doctors guide the local medical team for treatment decisions.
  6. Continuous monitoring improves patient safety and recovery.

Benefits of e-ICU

The e-ICU system improves patient survival rates, ensures faster medical response and provides expert healthcare access even in areas where specialist doctors are not available.
